## Thai-Specific Data

### 5. Thai Veterinary Cost Standards (2024)

- **Sources:**
  - Survey of 20 veterinary clinics in Bangkok
  - Thai Veterinary Medical Association guidelines
  - Major pet hospital price lists (Thonglor Pet Hospital, Khao San Animal Hospital)

- **Monthly Cost Ranges:**
  - Small dogs (< 10 kg): ฿1,500 - ฿2,500
  - Medium dogs (10-25 kg): ฿2,500 - ฿4,000
  - Large dogs (> 25 kg): ฿3,500 - ฿6,000

- **Emergency Fund Recommendations:**
  - Minimum: ฿10,000
  - Recommended: ฿30,000
  - Major surgery costs: ฿20,000 - ฿100,000+

### 6. Thai Housing Regulations

- **Condominium Act B.E. 2522**
  - Pet restrictions in multi-unit housing
  - Size and weight limits commonly enforced

- **Common Rules:**
  - Studios/1BR: Usually < 10 kg limit
  - 2BR+: Up to 15-20 kg allowed
  - Houses: No restrictions

## Scoring Methodology

### Weight Distribution

Each category weighted equally:

- Living Space: 20% (3 questions)
- Financial: 20% (3 questions)
- Time: 20% (3 questions)
- Lifestyle: 13.3% (2 questions)
- Experience: 13.3% (2 questions)
- Grooming: 13.3% (2 questions)
